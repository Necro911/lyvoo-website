We are GLP-Friendly

On GLPs?
We've got you covered.

GLP-1/GIP/glucagon agonists are a revolution, but rapid weight loss brings challenges many healthcare professionals are still not closely monitoring: muscle mass loss, nutritional deficiencies, renal and thyroid changes. Lyvoo is up to date.

OzempicWegovyMounjaroRybelsusSaxendaVictozaTrulicityRetatrutide
Muscle mass preservation
GLPs suppress appetite and muscle loss is common. We monitor body composition biomarkers.
Silent nutritional deficiencies
With reduced food intake, it is common to develop nutritional deficiencies. Lyvoo can identify this before symptoms appear.
Renal and hepatic function in focus
Dehydration and the drug's direct impact require periodic renal monitoring. The renal and hepatic panel is part of our protocol.
Real-time metabolic profile
HbA1c, fasting insulin and glucose. We track the metabolic response to treatment and adjust your plan with clinical precision.
