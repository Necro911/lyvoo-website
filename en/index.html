We are GLP-Friendly

On GLPs?
We've got you covered.

GLP-1/GIP/glucagon agonists are a revolution, but rapid weight loss brings challenges many healthcare professionals are still not closely monitoring: muscle mass loss, nutritional deficiencies, renal and thyroid changes. Lyvoo was built for exactly this.

OzempicWegovyMounjaroRybelsusSaxendaVictozaTrulicityRetatrutide
Muscle mass preservation
GLPs suppress appetite. Without adequate support, significant muscle loss is common. We monitor creatinine, urea and body composition markers.
Silent nutritional deficiencies
With reduced food intake, Vitamin B12, D, Iron, Zinc and Folate drop rapidly. The Lyvoo panel detects this before symptoms appear.
Renal and hepatic function in focus
Frequent dehydration and the drug's direct impact require periodic renal monitoring. Creatinine, eGFR, ALT and AST are part of our GLP protocol.
Real-time metabolic profile
HbA1c, fasting insulin, HOMA-IR and glucose. We track the metabolic response to treatment to adjust your dietary plan with clinical precision.
