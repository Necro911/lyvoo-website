How it works

From test to plan

Four simple steps, from sign-up to continuous health optimisation.

1
Become a member Today

Subscribe and receive your kit at home.

One annual programme, everything included. The collection kit arrives at your address within 48 hours, with everything you need for at-home testing.

  • Single annual payment, no surprises
  • Medical supervision included
  • Cancel at any time
2
Collect at home Day 1

Collect your sample. No needles, no travel.

A simple finger-prick collects your sample in 5 minutes. Follow the app instructions and return in the pre-paid envelope included.

  • No needles or venepuncture
  • 5-minute collection from the comfort of home
  • Pre-paid return to certified laboratory
3
Integrated interpretation 7-10 days

Medical report. Nutrition consultation.

When your results are ready, you receive a report with real clinical interpretation of each biomarker. We also schedule a nutrition consultation to turn your results into concrete action.

  • Medical interpretation of every out-of-range biomarker
  • Nutrition consultation scheduled after results are ready
  • Report in the app in clear, accessible language
4
Track and optimise 6 months

A plan that evolves with you. Reassessed.

After 6 months you repeat the panel and we compare results against your baseline to prove the progress.

  • 6-month reassessment with a new full panel
  • Comparative report: baseline vs. 6 months

Personalised health prevention.
From home. Made in Portugal.
