What it is

Extended screening, discreet and complete.

This module combines a blood sample and a urine sample to screen for the most relevant sexually transmitted infections, including HIV, Hepatitis B, Syphilis, Gonorrhoea, Chlamydia, Trichomoniasis, Herpes (HSV-1 and HSV-2) and other agents such as Mycoplasma and Ureaplasma.

Clear results, with a recommendation for specialist medical follow-up whenever needed.

Who it's for

For those who value knowing, privately.

Recommended for periodic screening, before or after new relationships, or for anyone who wants a complete sexual health check-up from home.

All collection and result delivery is handled privately through the Lyvoo app.
