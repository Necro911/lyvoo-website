Optional module

Extra Medical
Consultation

An additional preventive medical session with your Lyvoo team doctor for review or specific questions.

€89 per session
What it is

Your doctor.
When you need one.

After the initial Lyvoo assessment, the need for additional follow-up may arise - to review follow-up results, discuss a new health concern, or adjust the preventive plan to life changes.

This module gives you access to an extra session with your Lyvoo team's preventive doctor, without needing to renew the programme or wait for the six-month reassessment.

Who it's for

For those who want more than the standard.

Ideal for those who received results that raise questions, have been through a significant health change, want to discuss introducing supplementation, or simply want a second preventive medical opinion.

Also indicated for Lyvoo programme members who occasionally want a deeper level of medical follow-up.

What's included

45-minute session by video call
Preventive medical consultation in remote format, with prior access to your Lyvoo history by the doctor.
Review of your results
The doctor analyses your most recent results and biomarker history before the session.
Preventive plan adjustment
Updated medical recommendations and any adjustments to the preventive health plan after the session.
Written consultation summary
Summary of conclusions and recommendations delivered to the Lyvoo app after the consultation.
No waiting time
Booking available within 5 working days of module activation.
Referral if necessary
If the doctor identifies the need for an in-person consultation or additional test, they will guide and refer you.

Book your session.

Available for Lyvoo members. Add to your plan and receive your booking link within 24 hours.
