Nutrigenetics

DNA analysis to understand how your body responds to nutrients, metabolises fats, and your cardiometabolic predispositions.

What it is

Your unique biology,
decoded.

Each person metabolises food differently. The Nutrigenetics module analyses genetic variants that determine how your body processes carbohydrates, saturated fats, vitamins, and essential minerals.

Based on your genetic profile, the Lyvoo team personalises nutritional recommendations with a level of precision that blood tests alone cannot offer.

Who it's for

Ideal if you want to go deeper.

Recommended for those who have completed at least one Lyvoo assessment and want to understand the genetic reasons behind their laboratory results - or optimise nutrition before problems arise.

Particularly relevant in cases of resistance to weight loss, persistently elevated cholesterol, or fatigue with no apparent cause in laboratory results.

What's included

DNA collection kit
Buccal swab sent to your home. Simple collection, no additional blood required.
Detailed genetic report
Analysis of genetic variants related to metabolism, inflammation, vitamins, and cardiometabolic predispositions.
Integrated clinical interpretation
Genetic data is cross-referenced with your laboratory results for truly personalised recommendations.
Nutritional plan adjustment
Your dietary plan is reviewed and adapted based on your genetic profile by the Lyvoo nutritionist.
Results in 3 to 4 weeks
Certified laboratory genetic analysis with standard delivery timeframe to the Lyvoo app.
Clarification session
20-minute session with the clinical team to explain results and answer questions.
