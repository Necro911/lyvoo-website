What it is

Stress, measured.

Cortisol is the main stress hormone. Chronically elevated, it impairs sleep, immunity, metabolism and hormonal balance.

This module measures morning cortisol, the moment of greatest clinical relevance for assessing regulation of the stress axis.

Who it's for

For those feeling burnt out.

Recommended for anyone suffering from persistent fatigue, sleep difficulties, anxiety or a sense of burnout, who wants to understand the role of cortisol in these symptoms.

An objective first step to tackling chronic stress based on data.
