Complete gut flora analysis. Understand the impact of your microbiome on metabolic health, immunity, and wellbeing.

What it is

The second brain.
Analysed in depth.

The gut microbiome - the community of bacteria living in your intestine - directly influences metabolism, immune response, inflammation levels, and even mood. An imbalanced flora can explain fatigue, difficult digestion, weight gain, or chronic low-grade inflammation.

This module analyses the composition and diversity of your gut flora through a stool sample, identifying imbalances and nutritional intervention opportunities.

Who it's for

When blood numbers don't explain everything.

Recommended if you suffer from irregular digestion, irritable bowel syndrome, fatigue with no apparent cause, frequent food allergies, or persistent inflammation in laboratory results.

Also indicated for those wanting to optimise the microbiota before an intensive nutritional intervention or targeted probiotic supplementation.

What's included

Stool collection kit
Sent to your home with detailed instructions. Simple, discreet collection with a prepaid return envelope.
Bacterial diversity analysis
16S rRNA sequencing to map the main bacterial groups and assess the flora diversity index.
Intestinal permeability markers

Interpreted report

Intestinal intervention plan

Results in 2 to 3 weeks
