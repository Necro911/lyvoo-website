What it is

Your hormonal health.
Measured and understood.

Testosterone levels decline gradually from the age of 30, often without obvious symptoms. Fatigue, loss of muscle mass, low libido, mood changes, and difficulty concentrating can all be signs of hormonal imbalance - frequently overlooked in routine tests.

This module offers a complete assessment of male androgenic health, with clinical interpretation integrated into your other results.

Who it's for

For men who want to understand their hormonal baseline.

Recommended for men over 30 experiencing symptoms of androgenic decline, athletes wanting to optimise performance and recovery, or anyone who wants a complete hormonal baseline as a reference for the future.

Particularly relevant in cases of unexplained fatigue, loss of libido, or difficulty gaining muscle despite consistent training.

What's included

Complete male hormonal panel
Analysis of total and free testosterone, DHEA-S, LH, FSH, oestradiol, and PSA from a DBS sample.
Report with medical interpretation
Each marker contextualised clinically, with reference to functional values (not just laboratory ranges).
Prostate screening (PSA)
Included as a preventive marker with clinical interpretation appropriate to age and family history.
Cortisol and chronic stress
Assessment of the impact of cortisol on testosterone levels and energy.
Hormonal optimisation plan
Nutrition, supplementation, and lifestyle recommendations to support healthy hormonal levels naturally.
Results in 7 to 10 days
Certified laboratory analysis with delivery to the Lyvoo app.
